Sanctions applied by the European Commission to American companies

During 2008-2019, the European Commission blamed several American companies like Microsoft, Facebook, Google, Qualcomm and Apple for violation of the Competition Act and imposed significant fines. The total amount of fees is more than 10 billion euros. It should be noted that the decisions to apply fines were made directly by the European Commission. No court took part in making this decision.

The sanctions may be proven illegal in relation to the federal Racketeering Influenced and Corrupt Organizations (RICO) law (Public law 91-452). The European Commission has violated legal doctrines and is corrupt body. Such conclusion was made as the result of our investigation of a case of corruption (more specifically of Schengen visa trafficking) which was held from 2012 to 2019. Our dossier consists of more than 1000 pages.

Based on our dossier, the sanctions of the European Commission can be challenged in an American court.
It should be noted that sanctions caused damage to the interests of thousands American shareholders. Besides that, the budget of the USA did not receive significant income as taxes.
